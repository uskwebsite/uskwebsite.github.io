Fortunately HTML5 introduced new structurally based elements, including the and elements.
All of these new elements are intended to give meaning to the organization of our pages and improve our structural semantics. They are all block-level elements and do not have any implied position or style.
Additionally, all of these elements may be used multiple times per page, so long as each use reflects the proper semantic meaning.
